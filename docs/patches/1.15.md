## TL;DR — what actually changes how you play

- **New hero: Ikra** — long-range splash mage (Carry/Mid). Suspend projectile, armable
  slowing bubbles that detonate for big AoE, a cheap poke beam that pays bonus gold on
  kills, and a channel→twister knock-up ult. A poke-and-zone control mage.
- **Daybreak V6 map rework** — clearer jungle, more rotation/dive paths, redesigned Orb
  Prime pit, and rebalanced shrine/buff timers + gold/XP. Rotation timing and objective
  tempo shift; first objectives come a little later.
- **Jungle is harder to clear** — most camps are 5 CS, and camp leaders + Red/Blue deal
  current-health damage, so clears hit harder and you finish lower (the Hunt slay heal offsets
  some). The uniform 5 CS also makes the enemy jungler's farm easy to count — read their level,
  spikes, and ult/CC off it, and time invades/objectives around it.
- **Comeback XP** triggers later (1.5→1.75 gap), starts smaller, ramps harder.
- **Towers** got a 0.25s target-switch delay + reset-on-swap → predictable diving/farming.
- **Crits are pseudo-random now** — misses raise your next crit chance; crits lower it.
- **Eternals trimmed broadly**, with a few soft reworks (Krix anti-heal, Vesh mana-on-
  takedown, Marrow Jealousy → health-threshold damage amp, Vermis Crush immobilize burst).
- **Items:** Earth Spirit → **Gamma Gloop** (new tank crest); **Rebirth** reworked into a
  fighter resurrect item; carry/fighter/tank items reshuffled.

## Systems & map

- **Daybreak V6:** clearer jungle corners, more rotation/dive routes, redesigned Orb Prime pit;
  shrines spawn/respawn 120s→240s with scaling HP; Gold/Cyan buffs cheaper-HP, faster initial
  spawn, reshuffled gold/XP, second buff in duo/off lanes; Red/Blue + all camps retuned.
- **Jungle:** standard/Red/Blue camps = 5 CS; camp leaders deal current-HP damage; Red/Blue base
  damage 36→20 (+10/lvl) + 5% current-HP; Hunt slay restores 90 (+15/lvl) HP; jungle bugs swapped
  spawns, respawn 120→180s, much more HP + 10 armor.
- **Comeback XP:** gap 1.5→1.75, start 25%→10%, per-level 0.3→0.35, cap 4→4.35.
- **Tower:** 0.25s target-switch delay + reset-on-swap; max-HP damage vs melee 41%→40%, ranged 68%→67%.
- **Crit:** purely random → pseudo-random dynamic (streak-correcting).
- **Minions:** Melee/Ranged max level 15→18, Siege/Super 15→60 with level gold scaling; AI fixes
  (no 5s ignore after aggro drop, break aggro out of lane, Seedling <10% execution, better last-hits).

## ARAM balance

Mode-only changes (single-lane, no recall reset). These do **not** touch the 5v5 kit numbers above.

**Systemic (the ones that move ARAM strategy):**
- **Gold Drip** flat 11/s → ramps 11→13 by level (every 80s) — full builds land sooner late, favoring scalers/hyper-carries.
- **Minion magical armor** ~0 → 1..15 (melee/ranged) and negative → 0..10 (siege) by level — magical poke-through-minions loses value late; physical/true unaffected.
- **Sustain/stack item interval** Alchemical Rod/Azure Core & Soul Chalice/Resolution 2s→4s, Orb of Growth/Enlightenment 1.5s→4s — stacking carries come online ~half as fast.

**Per-hero ARAM tuning** (damage / healing / damage-received multipliers): ~34 heroes nudged to flatten the tier list — 19 buffed, 11 nerfed, 4 mixed. Buffed includes Crunch, Khaimera, Gadget, Gideon, Iggy & Scorch, Renna, The Fey, Sparrow; nerfed includes Phase, Mourn, Zinx, Legion, Neon, Dekker, Steel, plus Countess/Feng Mao/Riktor/Sevarog (now take more damage). Full table in `data/patches/1.15.json` (`aram.heroes`) and on the published page.

## Ranked

1.15 closes **Split 4** and opens **Split 5** (runs until V1.18, ~18 weeks) with a hard reset at the top:
- Everyone re-places (5–10 placement games).
- **Diamond/Paragon MMR reset to Platinum** — the top of the ladder compresses at season start, so early lobbies are noisy/uneven.
- Split 4 reward: placements + 1 win earns the split banner/icon.
- 15 ranked wins this split unlocks the **Gatekeeper Terra** skin.

Climb read: grind placements early while the ladder is soft, then bank the 15 wins over the split.

## After go-live (numeric resim checklist)

Run once 1.15 is live on omeda.city (drops 2026-06-30). Per CLAUDE.md the snapshot itself is the
public omeda API (no creds); the pred.gg winrate/build half needs `PREDGG_CLIENT_ID/SECRET` in a
fresh session.

1. `npm run snapshot` — pull 1.15 hero base stats + items (adds Ikra; updates the item numbers above).
2. `npm run drift` — confirm the snapshot now reads as current.
3. `npm run refresh` — full chain (needs PREDGG creds): augments → buildstats → skills → aggregate
   → artifacts → matrix → agreement. Re-derives winrates/builds for the new meta.
4. Zero-API copy passes: `COPY_MODE=prepare npm run copy:prepare` → pred-scout-coach agent →
   `npm run copy:ingest`, then `npm run review:builds`.
5. `npm test` — harness green; commit.

Until then the engine's numeric base stays on the 2026-06-20 (pre-1.15) snapshot, and any 1.15
numeric output is pre-patch. The digest above is the honest, committed trend layer in the interim.
